Pasta with mushrooms

Home

Description

This simple dish can become your comfort food. Dried mushrooms give it rich and pronounced flavour.

Suitable for vegans.

Ingredients

  • 25g dried mushrooms (any type);
  • 150g pasta (any type);
  • olive oil;
  • 3 black peppercorns;
  • 1 bay leaf;
  • 2 cardamom pods;
  • salt to taste.

Steps

  1. Get a large bowl with cold water and soak the mushrooms for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat a pan and add olive oil, black pepper, bay leaf, cardamom, and mushrooms with a bit of mushroom water once heated.
  3. Cover the pan with a lid and cook for 5 minutes.
  4. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil; cook the pasta in boiling water until nearly tender.
  5. Add the pasta from the pot to the pan with mushrooms, mix them together, and cook with the lid off for a few minutes (until fully cooked).
  6. Enjoy your pasta!
