Pasta with mushrooms

Home

Description

This simple dish can become your comfort food. Dried mushrooms give it rich and pronounced flavour.

Suitable for vegans.

Ingredients

  • 25g dried mushrooms (any type);
  • 150g pasta (any type);
  • olive oil;
  • 3 black peppercorns;
  • 1 bay leaf;
  • 2 cardamom pods;
  • salt to taste.
