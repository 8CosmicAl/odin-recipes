Vegan Japanese Spinach Salad

Home

Description

This vegan Japanese salad is a great substitute for an Asian seaweed salad.

Spinach should be bright green, not overcooked.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds, divided;
  • 2 tablespoons water;
  • 1Â½ tablespoons soy sauce;
  • Â½ teaspoon white sugar;
  • Â½ package (227 grams) of fresh spinach;
  • Pinch of salt.

Steps

  1. Place 1Â½ tablespoons sesame seeds on a plate.
  2. Use the bottom of a pot or a heavy measuring cup to crush the seeds.
  3. Transfer crushed seeds to a bowl.
  4. Add water, soy sauce and sugar; stir to combine.
  5. Bring a small pot of water to a boil; add salt.
  6. Add spinach and cook until wilted, no more than 1 minute.
  7. Drain spinach into a colander and rinse with cool water to stop the cooking process.
  8. Squeeze the spinach with hands to remove moisture.
  9. Cut spinach into strips.
  10. Place cooked spinach in serving bowls.
  11. Drizzle dressing on top and add remaining sesame seeds as garnish.
