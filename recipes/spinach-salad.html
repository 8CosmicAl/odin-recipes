Vegan Japanese Spinach Salad

Home

Description

This vegan Japanese salad is a great substitute for an Asian seaweed salad.

Spinach should be bright green, not overcooked.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons toasted sesame seeds, divided;
  • 2 tablespoons water;
  • 1½ tablespoons soy sauce;
  • ½ teaspoon white sugar;
  • ½ package (227 grams) of fresh spinach;
  • Pinch of salt.
