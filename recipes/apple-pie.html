Naturally Sweet Apple Pie

Home

Description

This is a rustic-style apple pie or apple cake hybrid — simple, wholesome, and free of added sugar and butter.

You can adjust natural sweetness by using riper apples or adding a small amount of honey or maple syrup.

Ingredients

Batter (liquid dough):

  • 2 large eggs;
  • 200 ml milk (dairy or plant-based like oat or almond);
  • 100 ml neutral oil (like sunflower or light olive oil);
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract (optional);
  • 200g flour (all-purpose);
  • 1 tsp baking powder;
  • pinch of salt;
  • optional: 1 mashed ripe banana OR 2 tbsp date paste for extra sweetness.

Filling:

  • 3–4 medium apples (preferably sweet varieties like Gala, Fuji, or Pink Lady), thinly sliced;
  • 1 tsp cinnamon (optional);
  • juice of ½ lemon;
  • optional: a handful of chopped raisins or dates for natural sweetness.
